How We Got Here

Built From
Experience,
Not Theory.

Apex Growth was founded in 2018 by a team of marketers who were frustrated with one thing: agencies that overpromised and underdelivered. We came from inside the industries we serve — we'd been real estate agents, fitness coaches, and startup operators ourselves.

We knew the pain of wasted ad spend, invisible brands, and leads that never converted. So we built the agency we wished had existed — one with deep niche expertise, obsessive attention to data, and a genuine stake in our clients' outcomes.

Today, Apex Growth operates with a team of 18 specialists across strategy, creative, paid media, and technology. Every client gets a dedicated growth team — not a junior account manager and a spreadsheet.

We measure our success in one currency: your revenue growth.
